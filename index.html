Welcome to ClockBuddyGenerator

The best free tool for teachers to pair students.

What is a clock buddy?

Clock buddies are custom partners generated for each student that correspond to the hours on the clock. When a teacher tells the class to "Get with your 3 O'Clock partner for this activity," each student will have a pre-assigned partner to meet with. This will save time partnering up and will encourage students to work with a variety of classmates, not just one or two different friends.



How does it work?

Our generators allow you to enter a list of students and customize the number of matches(or "hours") you want for each student, with the default set at 12. The generators will then use algorithms to ensure each student receives unique matches with no repeats. We even have a generator that allows student partner preferences and we do our best to match them.
